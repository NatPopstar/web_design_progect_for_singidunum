How to find us?

From the station University Singidunum

  1. 1

    Cross the road to the opposite side.

  2. 2

    Walk straight ahead down to the intersection.

  3. 3

    At the intersection, turn right.

    ☕ Landmark: there will be a café on your left.

  4. 4

    Walk straight ahead until you reach the large building of Singidunum University.

  5. 5

    Our office is located in this building.

    💬 Ask the security guard at the entrance — they will guide you.
